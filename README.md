<section id="about">
  <h2>About Us</h2>
  <p>
    Jawad Animal Welfare is a non-profit welfare initiative committed to the protection, care, and well-being of animals. Our mission is to reduce animal suffering through rescue, medical support, vaccination, and community awareness.
  </p>
  <p>
    Currently, we are actively running vaccination campaigns for livestock and animals, including cows, buffaloes, goats, donkeys, and dogs, to prevent the spread of dangerous diseases. Alongside vaccinations, we also provide basic veterinary treatments and first aid to animals in need, especially in underserved areas.
  </p>
  <p>
    Our long-term vision is to establish a dedicated animal shelter with a veterinary clinic, where stray animals and livestock can receive proper treatment, care, and rehabilitation in a safe environment.
  </p>
  <p>
    Jawad Animal Welfare believes that animals deserve compassion, respect, and protection. Through our ongoing welfare activities and future plans, we aim to build a healthier, more humane society for both animals and humans.
  </p>
