About Us

Jawad Animal Welfare is a community-led initiative providing free veterinary care, vaccines, and shelter for injured and sick working animals in rural Punjab, Pakistan. We aim to improve the lives of animals and support the livelihoods of low-income families that depend on them.

Our Services

  • Vaccination & deworming campaigns
  • Medical treatment & minor surgeries
  • Shelter for injured and abandoned animals
  • Farmer education on animal care
  • Emergency veterinary response during disease outbreaks
